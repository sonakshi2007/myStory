Once upon a time in a forest there lived a monkey who resided on a jamun tree which was on the banks of a river.
In a same the same forest there lived a crodile and his wife......
One day the crocodile came to the banks of river and rested under the tree.The kind hearted monkey offered him some fruits
The crocodile came back the next day for more fruits as he loved them.As the days passed by monkey and the crocodile became good friends.
